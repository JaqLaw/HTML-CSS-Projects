Montessori at Home

Ever thought of Montessori as something complicated, expensive or time-consuming? We're here to help change that.

Use what you have.

Montessori doesn't require expensive materials. Utilize what you have to create an environment that supports independence, intrinsic motivation and a love of learning.

Sweep, sweep, sweep!

Practical life is an important area in Montessori and often the first way toddlers can be independent. Sweeping with small hand brooms is perfect for little hands!

Alphabet Sound Sorting

It's easy to make a phonetic initial sound sorting game. Just find small objects around the house, make some letter cards and have your child match each object to its starting sound.

Flower Arranging
